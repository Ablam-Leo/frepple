=======
Segment
=======

Segments reprensent a collection of SKU that matches the criteria of the segment.
They can be used for filtering purpose to only display on the screen the SKU belonging to a given segment.
They can also be used in combination with one or multiple business rules to define a common inventory
policy to all SKU belonging to a segment.
